SERVICE PLEDGE

To show to our community that our barangay is clean peaceful and can provide to its people a fast service and efficient health and livelihood services.

To provide on updated, comprehensive, and focused description of all the aspect of life in the barangay.

Mission

To create and implement better programs and guidelines to protect the common interest of the community with regards to education, health, infrastructure, environment, peace and order, disaster preparedness towards a better quality of life for the populace.

Vission

A premier center of highly skilled and unified cityzenry, headed by decent leaders with high morals. A place with people possesing a great appreciation and fear of God Following existing laws; living in a peaceful and well developed community with a progressive economy, sustainable and disaster resillient environment.
